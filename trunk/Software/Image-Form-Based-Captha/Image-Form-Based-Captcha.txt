ABSTRACT:
=========
Method to prevent bots from identifying captcha images
by embedding the captcha image in a form created out 
of an Image itself. 

PURPOSE:
========
To defeat captcha programs with a near 100% success 
rate. 

DESCRIPTION:
============
The webform on a particular website is created out of
an image. The image has the embedded captcha image to
let the user authenticate as a valid user and not a 
bot program.
