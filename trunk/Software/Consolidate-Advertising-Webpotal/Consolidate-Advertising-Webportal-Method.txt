Abstract:
=========
A method to consolidate advertisements on a single website for
revenue generation and to advertise services provided a set of
companies/individuals.

Description:
============
Using a consolidated login management website to provide the 
service of advertisements of individuals/websites/internet-companies/
companies offering service via the internet registered with 
the login management website. So as the user registers with
the login management website, the user is also aware of other
service providing individuals/websites/companies...etc 

Purpose:
========
1. Revenue Generation
2. Advertising
